Goalkeeper (GK): Sweeper Keeper - Attack. Initiates counter-attacks, acts as 11th outfield player.
Right Back (RB): Complete Wing-Back - Support. Provides width, supports attacks, tracks back quickly.
Striker (ST): Pressing Forward - Attack. Leads pressing from front, forces defensive errors.

Tactical Instructions

In Possession
  • Play Out of Defence
  • Pass Into Space
  • Higher Tempo
  • Focus Through Middle
Out of Possession
  • Extremely Urgent Pressing
  • Much Higher Defensive Line
  • Prevent Short GK Distribution
  • Counter-Press Immediately
Transitions
  • Counter Immediately
  • Regroup Quickly
  • Distribute Quickly
Player Roles
  • Sweeper Keeper
  • Ball-Playing Defenders
  • Box-to-Box Midfielders
  • Pressing Forward
